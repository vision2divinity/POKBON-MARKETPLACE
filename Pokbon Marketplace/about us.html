About Us - POKBON Marketplace

Welcome to POKBON Marketplace, your trusted source for quality products in Ghana. We are a proudly Ghanaian-owned e-commerce platform,
committed to providing a wide range of affordable and high-demand items to meet the everyday needs of individuals and businesses.
At POKBON Marketplace, we believe in making shopping convenient, reliable, and accessible to everyone across the country.

Who We Are

POKBON Marketplace is a sole proprietorship registered in Accra, Ghana, under the trading name POKBON Marketplace.
operate with a focus on retail and wholesale distribution, offering a diverse range of products, including Home Appliances, Mother, Kids & Toys,
Kitchen Appliances, Office Gadgets, Luggage, Bags & Cases, TV Sets, Apparel & Accessories, and Consumer Electronics.
sourcing quality products directly from manufacturers, especially in China, we ensure that our customers get the best value at competitive prices.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to be a one-stop marketplace that simplifies your shopping experience.
We aim to bridge the gap between global suppliers and the Ghanaian market by bringing high-quality, essential products right to your doorstep.
POKBON Marketplace is dedicated to offering customer-centric services, making it easy for you to order, pay, and have your items delivered promptly and safely.

What We Offer

At POKBON Marketplace, we offer:

  • A Broad Selection of Products: From home essentials to cutting-edge electronics, our platform is tailored to suit the needs of individuals, families, and businesses.
  • Competitive Pricing: By partnering with reputable manufacturers and suppliers, we are able to offer competitive pricing on all products.
  • Convenient Shopping Experience: We’ve made it easy for you to browse, shop, and make secure payments on our user-friendly website. Our intuitive interface allows for quick navigation and a hassle-free checkout process.
  • Reliable Delivery Services: Whether you’re in Accra or any part of Ghana, we ensure your products are delivered on time. Our tracking system allows you to monitor your order every step of the way.
  • Customer Support: Your satisfaction is our priority. We provide excellent after-sales support, including easy returns and refunds policies, ensuring that you have peace of mind while shopping with us.
